ABC Technologies - Company Policy Document

1. Refund Policy

Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchasing a subscription.

To request a refund, customers must provide:

* Account ID
* Subscription details
* Reason for refund

Refund requests are reviewed by the billing team.

The AI support system cannot directly approve refunds. All refund requests must be sent to a supervisor for approval.

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

---

2. Subscription Cancellation Policy

Customers may cancel their subscription at any time.

After cancellation:

* No future payments will be charged.
* Current subscription benefits remain active until the billing cycle ends.

Cancellation requests require supervisor approval before processing.

---

3. Account Closure Policy

Customers can request permanent account closure.

Before closing an account:

* User identity must be verified.
* Any pending payments must be cleared.

Account closure requests are considered high-risk actions and require supervisor approval.

---

4. Customer Responsibilities

Customers are expected to:

* Keep login credentials secure.
* Provide accurate account information.
* Follow the company's terms of service.
* Report issues honestly.

The company is not responsible for losses caused by sharing passwords with others.

---

5. Privacy Policy

ABC Technologies respects customer privacy.

Customer information is collected only to:

* Manage accounts
* Process payments
* Provide support services

Customer data is not sold to third parties.

Only authorized employees can access customer information.

---

6. Data Retention Policy

Customer support conversations may be stored for future reference.

Billing records are stored for auditing purposes.

Closed account information may be retained for up to 90 days before deletion.

---

7. Support Response SLA

Response times depend on the subscription plan.

Basic Plan:
Response within 24 hours

Professional Plan:
Response within 8 hours

Enterprise Plan:
Response within 2 hours

Critical technical issues receive higher priority.

---

8. Escalation Policy

The following requests are automatically escalated to a supervisor:

* Refund requests
* Subscription cancellations
* Account closure requests
* Compensation requests
* Requests involving legal issues

The supervisor reviews the request before a final response is sent to the customer.
