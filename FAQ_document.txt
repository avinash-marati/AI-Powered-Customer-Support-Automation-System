ABC Technologies - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Version: 1.0

---

1. Account Related Questions

---

Q1. How do I reset my password?

A: Click the "Forgot Password" option on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

---

Q2. How do I change my account email address?

A: Go to Profile Settings and select "Update Email Address".

---

Q3. How do I update my profile information?

A: Navigate to Account Settings and edit the required fields.

---

Q4. Can I deactivate my account temporarily?

A: Yes. Contact the Account Support team for assistance.

---

Q5. How do I permanently close my account?

A: Submit an account closure request through customer support. Supervisor approval is required.

---

2. Subscription and Pricing Questions

---

Q6. What subscription plans are available?

A: ABC Technologies offers Basic, Professional, and Enterprise plans.

---

Q7. Can I upgrade my subscription plan?

A: Yes. You can upgrade your plan at any time from the subscription settings page.

---

Q8. Can I downgrade my subscription?

A: Yes. Downgrades become effective from the next billing cycle.

---

Q9. Do you offer a free trial?

A: Yes. New users receive a 14-day free trial.

---

Q10. Do annual subscriptions receive discounts?

A: Yes. Annual plans receive discounted pricing compared to monthly plans.

---

3. Billing Questions

---

Q11. What payment methods are accepted?

A: Credit Cards, Debit Cards, PayPal, and Bank Transfers for Enterprise customers.

---

Q12. How can I download my invoices?

A: Invoices can be downloaded from the Billing section of your account.

---

Q13. How do I request a refund?

A: Contact Billing Support with your subscription details and reason for the request.

---

Q14. How long does refund processing take?

A: Approved refunds are usually processed within 5-7 business days.

---

Q15. Why was my payment declined?

A: Payment failures may occur because of insufficient funds, expired cards, or bank restrictions.

---

4. Technical Support Questions

---

Q16. I forgot my password. What should I do?

A: Use the password reset option available on the login page.

---

Q17. My application crashes when uploading files.

A: Verify that the file size is below 100 MB and uses a supported format.

---

Q18. Which file formats are supported?

A: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PNG, and JPG.

---

Q19. What should I do if the application is running slowly?

A: Check your internet connection and clear your browser cache.

---

Q20. Which browsers are supported?

A: Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Safari.

---

Q21. What does Error Code 101 mean?

A: Error 101 indicates an invalid username or password.

---

Q22. What does Error Code 301 mean?

A: Error 301 indicates a database connection failure.

---

5. Security and Privacy Questions

---

Q23. Is my data secure?

A: Yes. Customer data is stored using industry-standard security practices.

---

Q24. Does ABC Technologies sell customer data?

A: No. Customer information is never sold to third parties.

---

Q25. Who can access my account information?

A: Only authorized employees can access customer information when required.

---

Q26. How long is customer data stored?

A: Account data may be retained for up to 90 days after account closure.

---

6. Support Questions

---

Q27. How can I contact customer support?

A: Customers can contact support through email, chat, or the support portal.

---

Q28. What are support working hours?

A: Support is available Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

---

Q29. How quickly will I receive a response?

A: Response times depend on your subscription plan.

Basic Plan: 24 hours

Professional Plan: 8 hours

Enterprise Plan: 2 hours

---

Q30. Which requests require supervisor approval?

A: Refund requests, account closure requests, subscription cancellations, compensation requests, and management escalations require supervisor approval.
