1. Overview

---

ABC Technologies provides cloud-based business management software for organizations of different sizes.

The company offers three subscription plans:

1. Basic Plan
2. Professional Plan
3. Enterprise Plan

---

---

$10 per month

Suitable For:

* Students
* Freelancers
* Small businesses

Features:

* Up to 5 users
* Basic reporting dashboard
* Email support
* 5 GB cloud storage
* Standard security features

Support Response Time:
Within 24 hours

8. Upgrade and Downgrade Policy

---

Customers can upgrade their subscription at any time.

Plan upgrades take effect immediately.

Plan downgrades are applied at the start of the next billing cycle.

---

9. Free Trial

---

New customers are eligible for a 14-day free trial.

No credit card is required during the trial period.

After the trial expires, customers may choose any available subscription plan.

---

10. Frequently Asked Pricing Questions

---

Q: Which plan is best for small businesses?

A: The Basic Plan is recommended for small businesses with limited users.

Q: Does the Enterprise Plan have fixed pricing?

A: No. Enterprise pricing depends on business requirements.

Q: Can I change my plan later?

A: Yes. Customers can upgrade or downgrade their subscription.

Q: Are there discounts for annual billing?

A: Yes. Annual subscriptions receive discounted pricing.
